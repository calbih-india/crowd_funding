About Us
Our Democracy is about raising awarness and a platform to bring change by the people for the people.

What is Our Democracy?

Our Democracy is a campaign community that helps journalists, political activists and changemakers build a community online that supports them financially and amplifies their campaign. It works in three broad areas:
1. Help make the media and public opinion ecosystem more credible
2. Make political funding transparent and our leaders accountable to the people.
3. Mobilise citizens to make social and political interventions when needed.

Our Politics

We believe our democracy is increasingly getting controlled by a very few number of people who are trying to control entire populations. We believe people need to come together and RECLAIM DEMOCRACY. The platform aims to create a level-playing field for changemakers and disruptors. It brings transparency and accountability to public life. We are committed to the ideas of social justice, gender equality, equal opportunity and a world that works for everyone. We believe love can conquer fear.
