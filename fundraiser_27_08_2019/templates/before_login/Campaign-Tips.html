Make your campaign fly in 7 steps

1
  • Share the campaign on Facebook & Twitter (Everyday with a different messaging)
  • Send the campaign to your WhatsApp contacts
  • Sent the campaign on your email list.
2
Share it on WhatsApp groups you are part of, asking people to help you spread the campaign.
3
Share the campaign with personalised messages to top 20 of your friends on Facebook in messenger, asking them to help & spread the word.
4
Call influencers, important people you know & tell them about your campaign, why you are doing it & why this is important. Send them the link to share it among their friends.
5
  • Make a small 2 minutes video, where you appeal people to support your campaign & post it with the campaign link on your Facebook & Twitter.
  • Send this video to people you think can help you in spreading the word.
6
Ask people who have supported your campaign if they can make a small video appeal asking people to support your campaign. Ask them to share it with the campaign link on their social media.
6
Send another round of WhatsApp, email & Facebook messages to everyone & ask them to support your campaign, or atleast spread it among their contacts.
