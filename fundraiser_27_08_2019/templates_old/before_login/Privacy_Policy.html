Privacy Policy
Right to Privacy is our right
Our Democracy values and respects the idea of protected user information and considers Privacy as an inalienable right of every user. Listed in this document are the various ways that Our Democracy registers your information, and uses them. This includes the information collected from you when you visit www.ourdemocracy.in. By visiting www.ourdemocracy.in, you are accepting the Terms and Conditions described in this Privacy Policy.
Information collected when you visit the Our Democracy platform
When you visit our website, you may provide us with personal information (such as name, address, e-mail address, telephone numbers and/or credit/debit card information) that you knowingly choose to disclose, which is collected on an individual basis for various purposes. These purposes include submitting a form on our website, or simply asking a question. We receive and store any information you enter on our website or give us in any other way.
We ask for personal information so that we can fulfill your request and reply to you. This information is retained and used in accordance with existing laws, rules, regulations, and other policies. Our Democracy does not collect personal information from you unless you provide it to us. If you choose not to provide any information, we may not be able to fulfill your request, but you will still be free to browse the other sections of the websites owned and operated by Our Democracy. This means that you can visit our site without telling us who you are or revealing any personally identifiable information about yourself.
We only use your information for the purpose we specify
When you supply information about yourself for a specific purpose, we use the information for only that purpose (such as to provide the information you have requested). For example, you may be asked to give us individual details to start a fundraiser. We use email addresses provided by you to send regular updates about the campaign, or to send you newsletters. You could however choose to opt out of receiving these newsletters by clicking on the unsubscribe button..
We use return email addresses to answer emails we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with any third parties.
Our Democracy does not sell, rent, give-away or share its e-mail addresses or other information with outside sources.
Ensuring data security
Personally identifiable information is stored on our server and is not publicly accessible. Further, personally identifiable information is only accessed by Our Democracy personnel on a "need to know" basis. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate security procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
IP numbers are not stored, but are temporarily used to determine domain type and in some cases, geographic region. We do not make any association between this information and a visitor's identity. Our Democracy uses cookies to identify your browser as you visit pages on the Our Democracy website or sites in the Our Democracy media network. Cookies created on your computer by using our website do not contain personally identifiable information and do not compromise your privacy or security. Cookies allow Our Democracy to gather anonymous information.
Online Registration Information
You will provide us with information about yourself and your organization when you register for fundraising or any other purpose. This information is not used for any other purpose than to fulfill your request and is not shared with outside parties. However, visitors should be aware that information collected through Our Democracy website may be subject to examination and inspection if such information is a public record or not otherwise protected from disclosure.
Links
This website may contain links to other websites. Please note that when you click on one of these links, you are entering another site. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of these linked websites as their privacy policy may differ from ours.
E-mail Links
We use e-mail links located on this site to allow you to contact us directly via e-mail. We use the information provided in your e-mail to respond to your questions or comments. We may also store your comments for future reference.
Changes to this Privacy Statement
Changing business practices and circumstances may require that we make changes to this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes made here will be reflected on our website. Our Democracy reserves the right to modify its website and/or this Privacy Policy at any time and users are deemed to be appraised of and bound by any such modifications. Our goal is to provide all services to visitors to the Our Democracy website in an accessible, efficient and friendly manner while maintaining their privacy. If you have comments or questions regarding privacy, please contact us.
Legal Disclaimer
We may disclose personal information when required by law or in the belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Our Democracy.
