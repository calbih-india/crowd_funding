About The Campaign

About the campaign 


We are very careful decision makers in almost every aspect of our personal lives. We look for experts, skills and qualifications when it comes to choosing a doctor for example for our health matters. We choose the best teachers and schools for our kids or for our own education. But what happens when it comes to making our biggest decision for shaping the future of our nation? What happens when it comes to choosing our People Leaders for Lok Sabha which houses the custodians and shapers of the present and the future of our motherland? At the time of choosing our future parliamentarians, we forget to look hard or dig deep into the credentials, track records, educational background and above all their motivation and commitment to serving people and preserving democracy.Here is an opportunity for you to change that. Choose your parliamentarians right and shape the future of India.Ghanshyam is a true People Leader, committed to serving the masses, the underprivileged, the undereducated and above all the motherland; selfless and tireless in his pursuit of solving real issues of people, issues of education, skill development and job creation. He is one of the most qualified youth leaders of today you can find and more importantly trust to give voice and force to your thoughts, your issues and your dreams.Be the catalyst for the change you want to see in the Lok Sabha and in your Parliamentarians. Donate with your heart, keeping the future of our Nation in mind.Why Ghanshyam as a future ParliamentarianGhanshyam, a 39 years old youth leader comes from a little known village called Gorari, housed in the Karakat constituency of Bihar. This is the constituency that has now become Ghanshyam’s battleground for Lok Sabha.Ghanshyam grew up in a modest household with a Fauzi father and a mother who taught primary school children. His father’s armed forces background and transferable job, aided Ghanshyam’s naturally enterprising self to travel to various towns of India, learning and enriching his knowledge of our motherland. Passionate from the beginning, Ghanshyam graduated from NIT Surathkal and went onto complete his post-graduation from the Kellogg School of Management and the Harvard University. With a deep-rooted inner drive to serve the motherland and inspired by numerous interactions with the world leaders, Kellogg and Harvard professors and like-minded fellow colleagues he solidified his life’s mission – i.e., to dedicate his thoughts, actions and work life to his birth land Karakat and to nation building, at large.Post Harvard, Ghanshyam has spent over 10 years on ground in Bihar and UP politics. Ghanshyam started out by undertaking grassroots level work, right here in the hinterlands of Bihar. More recently Ghanshyam has moved to national political arena, in his role as at the national spokesperson of the Samajvadi Party. He is regarded as one of the most thought provoking, people-oriented and yet fierce voices in television debates on all national channels. He has finally become the voice of masses on mass media. He is now seeking your support to become a people leader shaping the decisions of tomorrow.Ghanshyam’s journey which is nurtured with honesty, trust and outright selflessness needs your help with funding.Ghanshyam will be running for the Lok Sabha seat from Karakat and your support will go a long way in ensuring that the true passion of this young contender and the dreams of many of those who want to see a real change in our country, do not fall short.
